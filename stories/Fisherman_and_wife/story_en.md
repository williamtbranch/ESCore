Once there was a man who lived with his wife by the sea. They lived in a very small and very old house. The man went to the sea every day. He went to look for fish.

One day, the man was sitting by the water. He looked at the water and waited. Then he pulled a very big fish out of the water. But the fish spoke. «Please, let me live», said the fish. «I am not a fish like the others. Put me back in the water and let me go.» The man was afraid. «I don't want a fish that talks», he said. «Go away.» And he put the fish back in the water. The fish went down to the bottom of the sea.

The man went back to his house. He told his wife everything. He told her that he had pulled out a very big fish, and that the fish talked, and that he had let it go. «Didn't you ask it for anything?», said the wife. «We live very badly here, in this small and old house. You have to go to the sea again. I want the fish to give us a good house.»

The man did not like the idea, but he went to the sea. The water was at peace. The man stood by the water and said:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

The fish came. «What does your wife want?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «my wife doesn't want to live in that old house anymore. She wants a good house.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «She already has her good house.»

The man went back. His wife was at the door of a good house. «Come in, come in», she said. «Isn't this much better than the other house?» Inside, everything was good. Near the house there was a very good place with many animals. «Ah», said the man, «now we are going to live well.» «We'll see», said the wife.

A little time passed. One day, the wife said: «This house is very small for the two of us. I want a much bigger house. I want a big house like a king's house. You have to go to the fish again.» «Wife», said the man, «I don't want to go back to the fish again. The fish won't like it. This good house is fine now.» «No», said the wife. «The fish will do it. You have to go.»

The man went, but he didn't want to go. The water of the sea was no longer so at peace. The man stood by the water and said:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

«What does she want now?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «my wife wants a big house like a king's house.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «She is already at the door.»

The man went back and found his wife at the door of a very big house, like a king's house. «Look», she said. «Isn't it very big and good?» They went in together. Inside there were many people who helped them. There were things of gold everywhere. Near the house there was a very big place with many animals. «Good», said the man, «now we are going to live here very well all our lives.» «We'll see», said the wife. «First let's think about it.» And they went to sleep.

The next morning, the wife called the man. «It's already day», she said. «I want to be queen of all the land.» «Wife», said the man, «why do you want to be queen? I don't want to be king.» «Well, I do», she said. «You have to go to the fish. I want to be queen.» The man went, and he didn't want to go. The sea was no longer well. The man stood by the water and said:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

«What does she want now?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «my wife wants to be queen.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «She is already queen.»

The man went back. Now the house was much bigger. There were many men in the house. His wife was sitting up very high, in a place all of gold. She had gold on her head. «Wife», said the man, «are you queen now?» «Yes», she said. «I am queen.» The man looked at her for a long time. «Ah, wife», he said, «how good it is to be queen. Now we won't want anything more.» «I don't know», she said. «I am queen, yes, but I don't like only this anymore. I want to be greater. I want to rule over all the lands of the world.» «Wife», said the man, «why do you want more? Being queen is fine now.» «No», she said. «You have to go to the fish. I want to be the greatest queen in all the world.»

The man had to go. On the way he said to himself: «This is going to turn out badly. It is too much. One day the fish won't want any more.» When he got to the sea, the water was very high and very bad. The man stood by the water and said:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

«What does she want now?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «my wife wants to be the greatest queen in all the world.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «She already is.»

The man went back. His wife was sitting in a place of gold, very high. She had something very big of gold on her head. On both sides there were many people. Near her there were important men. «Wife», said the man, «are you the greatest queen in the world now?» «Yes», she said. «I am.» «Ah», said the man, «how good.» «Husband», she said, «why stop here? Now I want to rule over everything. I want everyone to do what I say.» «Wife», he said, «that is too much.» «No», she said. «You have to go to the fish. I want to rule over everything.»

The man went. But now the sea was very bad. The water was very high and very bad. Everything turned very bad, like before something very bad. The man was very afraid, but he stood by the water and said:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

«What does she want now?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «my wife wants to rule over the whole world.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «She already rules over everything.»

The man went back and found his wife sitting up very high. She had three things of gold on her head. On both sides of her there were many things, some very big and others very small. «Wife», said the man, «do you rule over everything now?» «Yes», she said. «Good», he said, «now you can't be any greater. Now you will be at peace.» «I'll think about it», said the wife.

They went to sleep, but the wife could not sleep. She was thinking about what more she wanted to be. When the sun came up, the wife looked at it and felt very bad. She called the man. «Husband», she said, «you have to go to the fish. I want to rule over the sun and the moon.» The man was so afraid that he didn't know what to do. «Wife», he said, «can't you be at peace with what you already have?» «No», she said. «I am not at peace while the sun and the moon come up without my saying so. You have to go to the fish right now!»

The man went, very afraid. As he went toward the sea, the sea turned very bad. Everything turned very bad and very black. The water of the sea was very high and very bad. The man reached the sea and said, as best he could:

«Fish of the sea, fish of the sea,
come to me.
My wife wants something.
I ask you for it.»

«What does she want now?», said the fish. «Ah», said the man, «she wants to rule over the sun and the moon.» «You can go to your house now», said the fish. «Now you are going to go back to your small and old house.»

And there the two of them live to this very day.
