Chapter One.
Say that my name is Ishmael. Some time before now, it does not matter how long, I had very few things and there was nothing that I wanted to do. So I thought about going to the sea, because the sea is the biggest water of all. I wanted to go for a short time to see the water part of the earth.
I do this when I want to feel good and when all the things inside me feel black, like a cold and wet day with no sun. When I walk to all the places where they keep the dead, and more than all, when I feel so bad that I want to do bad things to the people in the street, then I know that it is the time to go to the sea very soon.
Going to the sea is what I do so I do not do worse things. Other men do bad things to their own bodies, but I do not make any sound and I go to the ship, that big house that moves on the water. I go to the ship and there is nothing strange in this. If they knew it, almost all men at some moment feel the same thing that I feel about the sea.
Look at the city, that big place of many houses and roads. The water is on all sides around the city, and all the roads take you toward the water. Go to the edge where the water moves against the stones and look at all the people standing there, looking at the sea.
Walk through the city in the middle of a day with no sound when little is happening and the head goes where it wants to go by itself. Go from one side of the water to the other side and tell me what you see. You see many people standing without moving, looking at the ocean and lost in their own thoughts. Some are standing next to the wood, others sit at one side of the water, and others look at the ships. But all of these are men of the land who on other days are inside, at their work, and are not free to leave. So, what is this and why are they all here at the water?
And look that more people come walking straight toward the water, as if they wanted to fall inside. Nothing makes them happy more than the side of the land where the water and the land touch. They come from all places, from the north and the south, from the east and the west, but they all come together here at the water. Tell me what the sea has that brings them here.
Think about it another way, and say that you are far in the land where the land goes up to the mountains and the waters are small. Take the road that you want and almost always it will take you down to the water, to a small water or a river. There is something in this, because if you take a man who is lost in his thoughts and you put him standing to walk, he will take you to the water if there is water in that place. People and water go together, and everyone knows that when a man wants to think or feel, he goes to the water.
Now, when I say that I go to the sea, I do not mean that I go as a passenger, one of those people who gives money and does no work. To go as a passenger you need money and I did not have money. And also, after a time the passengers do not feel good on the water. Their bodies feel bad, they do not sleep well and they are not happy, so no, I never go as a passenger.
And I do not go as a captain, that big man who tells everyone on the ship what to do, nor do I go as the man who makes the food for everyone. I do not go as a man above others, because I leave that to the men who want it. For me it is enough work to take care of myself and I do not want to take care of a whole ship.
When I go to the sea, I go as a simple sailor, a man who works on a ship with his hands, down near the front part of the ship. It is true that they tell me what to do and they move me from here to there all day, and at the start this does not feel good because a man feels small. Especially if he comes from a good family or if before he was a big man who said words to children who thought that he was important, going from that to being a sailor is a hard change, but after a time the bad feeling goes away.
And what about this? If an old captain tells me to wash the wood of the ship, what does it really matter and does that make me less of a man? To all people in some way another person tells them what to do, and we are all moved one way or another. So I do my work as a sailor and it is fine for me.
And there is another thing, because when I go as a sailor they pay me and they give me money. They never pay a passenger, because the passenger has to give money to them, and there is a big difference between giving money and being given money. Giving your money is one of the hardest things in life, but being given money is something without equal.
And one more thing: as a sailor I live in the good air in the front part of the ship. Why, after all my times at the sea on work ships, did I decide to go on a ship that hunts whales? A whale, oh, that big animal of the sea! Why did I want to go on a trip to look for them, in that long time moving on the sea? I cannot say it completely.
Something that I cannot see seems to move me, take care of me and take me to where it wants. I think that this whale trip was always going to happen to me, because it was written for me a long time ago as a small part in a much bigger story.
More than all, what moved me was the thought of the big whale itself. An animal so big and strange made me want to see it for myself, and I thought about the far seas where it moves its body the size of a mountain, and about the many bad things that have no name.
Other men maybe do not want those things, but I am a man who always wants what is far away. I love to sail seas where no one should go and to put my feet on new lands, so the whale trip was something good for me. The big doors of a world full of new things opened for me, and through them came many whales two by two, and in the middle of all of them, a big white shape like a mountain of snow standing in the air.
