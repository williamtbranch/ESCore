Chapter 1. Signs.

My name is Ishmael. Some years ago I had little money and nothing on land gave me desire to stay. So I thought: I am going to go out to sea for a time. I always do that when inside I am not well. There are days when everything goes badly. I do not want to speak with anyone. I go out walking and I feel bad inside. On those days, so as not to fall further, I look for the sea. For me, going to sea is not a game. It is a way to put my head in order and be well again.

Look at the city beside the water. Many paths go toward the port. On the side of the water there are many people looking at the sea. Some are standing. Others sit down. Others look at the boats without saying anything. All come from homes and from places of work. But in the end they all arrive at the same place: where the water begins. They come from all sides. And there they stay, as if they were waiting for something.

Something similar happens in other places. You take a path and that path goes down to the water. It is as if people, when they think a lot, look for water without knowing it. If someone thinks a lot and starts to walk, he ends near the water. Water and life too. There is something there that we all feel, even if we do not know how to say it well.

Think of a person who wants to see a very good and very peaceful place. He can put a house, land, and a long path. He can put sun and people over there. Everything can look fine. But if there is no water there, something is missing. The eyes look for it. What is inside looks for it too. That is why I say that water and thinking go together. When a person looks at life and thinks a lot, he almost always also looks for the water.

And that does not happen only once. Again and again I see people go to the water. They go little by little. They arrive and they stay. They look at the sea a long time. Some do not speak. Some go alone. Others go with others. But they all seem to want the same thing: to be near the water for a moment and to let what is inside settle a little. Maybe they cannot say well why they go. They go anyway.

I am the same. When the land makes me feel bad, I look for the sea. When everything goes bad, I look for the sea. When I feel many things in my head, I look for the sea. There the water follows its course and I can follow mine. That is why I come back again and again. Not because it is easy, but because there I become myself again.

When I say that I go to sea, I am not speaking about going as a person who only goes and pays. I did not have money for that, and I did not want that place. I did not want to go as captain nor as the chief. I wanted to go as a sailor, with hands in the work and my face to the sea. At first that change is not easy. They tell you what to do all day. They take you from one side to another. They make you feel small. But with time one understands. The body understands the work and the head can be at peace.

And what does it matter if an older captain tells me what to do? In that moment it is not very pleasant, of course. No one wants to listen to another all day. But, if one thinks about it well, almost all of us live like this. There is always someone who says a little more. There is always someone who speaks first. So I do my part and carry on. If today my turn is to obey on the ship, it is no problem. That is how life is for almost everyone.

Besides, as a sailor they pay me. That part matters. The person who goes for pleasure pays. The sailor gets paid. One thing feels bad and the other feels good. And there is also the life of the ship. There the air hits your face and the body truly works. You end up wanting to sleep, but in a good way. Many times I thought that this life at sea did me more good than a life with no change on land.

And there is another thing about the sea that helps me. On the ship the day has a clear order. You stand up, work, eat, look at the water, and go back to the work. Then night comes and the body wants to sleep. So the head stops going in circles. It is not an easy life, but it is a clear life. To me that is worth a lot. That is why, when life on land turns very bad, I go back to the sea. There I feel that the day and the night have their place, and that I have mine too.

So, why, after so many voyages, did I think of a whaling ship? I do not fully know. I feel that something was leading me toward it from before. I believed it was an idea of my own, but maybe that idea already lived in me from a long time back. Sometimes life puts us on a path and one only understands afterward why one took that path.

Besides, I have always felt the urge to see what lies beyond. I like the great sea and going out for a long time. I do not look only for what is easy. What I do not know calls to me too. That is why whale hunting entered so strongly into my head. There was something big in me that wanted to see that world for myself: sea with no land near and the shape of a great whale over the water. I thought of many whales passing one after another, and among them all one white whale, very big, over the sea.
